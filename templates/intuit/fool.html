1. Intuitive Interpretation - The Fool
  2. Where did the The Fool come from? Where is The Fool going?
  3. A white Sun rises in the east; gaining momentum, energy. High above the horizon,
  4. but not directly overhead; not yet fully expressed. In process. One step ahead, The
  5. Fool leads the procession of White Light, bringing into being everything and more,
  6. containing every possibility, the source of pure potential. Immanent and
  7. Transcendent.
  8. Mountain Peaks, white like the Sun, project new heights of conscious awareness.
  9. Blue Mountains below a yellow Sky - the earth is lifted up! as The Fool ventures with
  10. lofty ambition into the Wilderness; the majestic, awesome power of uncultivated
  11. Life.
  12. Skin the color of Sky, neither what you would expect. The Fool is emanation,
  13. representation, manifestation of what Is above and beyond. Zero hovers overhead,
  14. surreal. All and Nothing.
  15. Headdress made of leaves – no crown, unbound. Leaves belong to trees, as
  16. alchemists, turning sunlight into nourishment. Elemental transmutation.
  17. Fool as fractal. Leaves surrounding yellow circles; lines, red like the Feather,
  18. dissecting. Into what? Feathers belong to birds, to aid in flight. From Sky to Earth
  19. and back again. The internalization of an external process, and vice versa.
  20. The Fool wears black? The Fool wears clothes? Particularly a garment so worn,
  21. tattered. More symbol than necessity, of the relationship between scarcity and
  22. abundance. An outer mystery, a shabby veil, intimates layers of experience. The
  23. Fool walks in tights the color of the Ground - stable, solid, fertile – treading in yellow
  24. shoes of celestial origins. The Sun-god, embodied, bringing light to the world.
  25. What does The Fool carry in Its Satchel? Why does It carry a Satchel at all? Small
  26. enough, it could not hold more than what is needed. The Fool is not worried; The
  27. Fool does not hoard.
  28. An Eagle’s head on the front of the Satchel, level with The Fool’s, faces east. Bird of
  29. prey, hierarchical tendency. The Fool leaves the past behind. Meanwhile, the past
  30. accompanies the present into the future. Perhaps The Fool is not as carefree as
  31. appearance would suggest - toting baggage, holding memories.
  32. Chin up, head back, moving westward. With graceful bearing, fine features, flowing
  33. movement, and delicate touch, The Fool responds to the prompting of the active
  34. principle and journeys into the unknown, engaging the trial that invokes self-
  35. confidence. Expansive Consciousness, Adventurous Spirit.
  36. A White Rose absorbing, containing, reflecting the light of the Sun. The Fool holds
  37. love, desire, romance – matters of the heart – sensuality, beauty – quality of
  38. experience and depth of emotion, lightly in Its left hand. Aspects of the human
  39. experience which exist in relation and reinforce attachment; The Fool is receptive
  40. but does not cling.
  41. Alone, but not lonely, a White Dog follows The Fool toward the edge of a precipice.
  42. The Dog offers loyalty despite the threat of danger, companionship in the unknown.
  43. Dog, Rose, Sun aligned with feet, heart, head – in faith, trust, boundless energy, pure
  44. intention.
