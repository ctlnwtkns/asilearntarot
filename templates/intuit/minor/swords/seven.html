Intuitive Interpretation

Seven of Swords

Lively, playful, mischevious, dangerous. This image conveys a sense of subversive action. An attempt to undermine the careful planning of a dominant presence, to disrupt the status quo. The central figure is flouting the rules, flaunting his disregard for rules and order.
