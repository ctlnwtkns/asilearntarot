Traditional Meanings

Regret for the past, fear of the future

  1. I believe the sense of foreboding and heaviness I mention in the first sentence echoes this sentiment.
  2. Regret for the past is fixation on a sense of failure and the futility of wishing something to be other than what it is. Fear of the future is that feeling of dread when you have to do something you do not want to do, or perhaps the anxiety of realizing you are alone in the dark.
  3. This interpretation seems pretty straightforward and I seem to intuitively agree.
